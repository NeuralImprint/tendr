Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I create an event? You can sign up and click on "Plan an Event".
  • Can I contact vendors directly? Yes, you can message them after shortlisting.
  • What’s the cost of using Tendr? Tendr is free to browse. Premium services vary by vendor.
  • How do I make a payment? Secure payment options are available at booking stage.
  • How do I become a vendor? Click on “Are you a business?” on the signup page.
